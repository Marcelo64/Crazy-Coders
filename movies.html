Movie recommendation for this Week

How about a horror movie on a hot day?

A patient goes missing at a psychiatric hospital in Shutter Island, Boston. Federal Sheriff Teddy Daniels (Leonardo DiCaprio) heads to Shutter Island with colleague Chuck (Mark Ruffalo) for an investigation. The hospital, located in Sher Tare Island, is a ward that quarantines mentally ill people who have committed serious crimes, and it is impossible to escape. But the woman accused of killing three children disappears with a strange note, and Teddy interrogates doctors, nurses and hospital officials for an investigation, but all they say is made up, and the investigation is not progressing at all.

The development itself is not very dynamic and it continues to cause subtle tension, so it may be a little boring depending on your taste, but it is a work with a clear and shocking storyline.
